Prashank Sharma

Professional Singer

Known for his unique vocal style, Prashank has captured the hearts of music lovers . With a voice that resonates with raw emotion and authenticity, Prashank has built a reputation for delivering powerful performances that connect deeply with audiences. Whether on stage or in the studio, Prashank's music tells stories of love, life, hope], drawing from personal experiences and universal truths

Biography

Prashank Sharma is a rising star in the world of music. Born in Lucknow, Uttar Pradesh, in 2000, Prashank always had a deep love for music, especially the soulful sounds of classic pop and contemporary R&B. From a young age, he was encouraged by his parents to explore his vocal talent, often performing in school talent shows and local events. His early influences include artists like Kishore Kumar,Arijit Singh which inspired him to develop a unique vocal style that blends rich emotion with sensibilities.
